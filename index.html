Club Membership and fees

For those joining for the first time a cheque for £50 is required - to cover the annual membership fee of £30 and a refundable key-deposit of £20. The key-deposit is returned when the member leaves the club.

If you join after the 31st August (in the current calendar year) the membership fee is reduced to £10.00 for the remainder of the year (so £30.00 total cost). For a member renewing existing membership (for another year), the cost is £30 only.

Box-league

The squash club runs a box-league for club members, which is set up to run at approximately two-monthly intervals.

The rules of the box-league can be read in the rules page. If there is space available in the current box-league we might be able to slot you in, but usually the boxes are full.
